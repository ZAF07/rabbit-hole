# Retention is earned through curiosity and investment, never gamified

Retention is driven by genuine curiosity and personal investment — **never by compulsion mechanics**. Streaks, badges, points, and leaderboards are **rejected outright, at every phase** — not deferred, not "maybe later." Compulsion machinery is the behavioral-design equivalent of AI slop: it manufactures engagement instead of earning it, and it would silently contradict the entire content discipline the product is built on.

**The retention model — three intrinsic drivers:**

1. **Direct — the daily curiosity hook (V1).** One dignified daily notification teasing the [Daily Feature](../../CONTEXT.md)'s real hook (*"Why does the US Navy still run on 1970s code?"*), held to the same anti-slop / anti-clickbait bar as in-app hooks — a genuine open loop the Piece closes. One a day, matching the Daily Feature heartbeat. Never a nag, never bait-and-switch.

2. **Indirect — the Tapestry (V1).** Users return because they are **building a personal intellectual trail they own** — their **Personal Knowledge Graph**: the growing graph of Pieces they've read and Connections they pulled, their per-user subgraph of the Content Graph induced by Session paths ([ADR 0008](0008-sessions-instrumented-from-v1.md)). This is *investment / endowment*, not compulsion. In V1 the Tapestry is **built and shown**; personalizing *from* it is a Phase-2 use of the same asset — one entity, two uses. **Free in V1; a candidate premium (paywalled) feature later.**

3. **Indirect — bounded depth journeys / Arcs (V2).** Finite, curated, finishable arcs with a stated learning goal — *"5 Pieces to understand how modern maps work,"* *"7 Pieces to why semiconductors are geopolitically critical."* People return for a **finishable intellectual object**. Infinite content is cheap; **completion of understanding is valuable.** The deliberate opposite of infinite scroll.

**Why:**
- The brand is intellectual **trust**; compulsion mechanics are manufactured, not earned, and reading as manipulation would poison the same well the closed-book grounding and anti-slop pipeline work to keep clean.
- Intrinsic retention **compounds honestly** — a better Piece and a richer Tapestry each make tomorrow more valuable with no dark pattern pulled.
- **Completion beats infinity.** A finishable arc creates the scarcity and satisfaction an endless feed structurally cannot; infinite feeds breed fatigue, finite arcs breed return.

**Trade-off:** earned retention is slower and harder than engagement-hacking — there is no cheap compulsion lever to pull. Accepted; that difficulty *is* the moat. An app that respects the user's mind is the product, not a growth tactic bolted onto it.

**Consequence:** no streak/badge/point/leaderboard system is ever built. Notification copy becomes an **editorial deliverable** held to the hook rubric, not a growth-team A/B surface. The Tapestry is a **V1 build** and depends on the path persistence established in [ADR 0008](0008-sessions-instrumented-from-v1.md). Arcs are a **V2 concept** needing a curation/authoring model, deferred.
